About Us

Who Are We?

We are a Hackathon team based in Washington that frequently participates in local hackathons. Our team consists of friends from different schools who all have a passion for computer science and are always up to the challenge of a hackathon.

Although Washington is known for its competitivness and high-programming skill level, our skills have landed us high places and prizes in a few hackathons which we participated in. We have shown that we can consistently win awards and get earnings.

We have participated in hackathons from Hackabyte, EMP, and other organizations in the Redmond/Kirkland Washington area.
